This is a stealth game where the player has to manouvere around the environment and reach the goal while also avoiding the enemies.

The enemies will follow a fixed patrol route where they will walk and stay in a position for a set time.

if the player is spotted by the enemies then the player character will imediately die.

There will be objects such as consoles the nplayer can interact woth to open doors and pickups such as keys.

player detection will be determined if the player enters the enemis line of sight.

Sound will also be a factor in player detection, if the player makes too much sound the surrounding enemis will be alerted.

Sprinting will emit more sound and crouching will emit no sound.
